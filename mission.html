Our Ethics: Morality-based

There exists a debate regarding the ethics and morals surrounding cybernetics. Concerns including privacy, harm, theft, and much more are addressed in the following bullet points:

  • We maintain secular standards, calling our employees to not uses biases stemmed from religious beliefs.
  • We implement guidelines that refrain from tracking.
  • We consult experts in purity and fair-based morals to ensure we have the best intentions in mind.
  • We conduct routine investigations to make sure no information is being stolen.
  • We run analyses on all of our employees every month to guarantee works that contribute to the greater-good of the world.
